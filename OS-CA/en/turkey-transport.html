OUR SERVICES

Submit Your Order – We Deliver

We offer all services related to shipping.

  • Export shipments to Turkey
  • Import shipments from Turkey
  • Partial and full loads (LTL / FTL)
  • Competent representation in Turkey
  • Intermodal Transport
  • Turkey Truck Transport (TIR)
  • Temperature-controlled transport according to regulations (LTL / FTL)
  • ADR, Hazardous Goods transport
  • Sprinter/Minivan Express shipments
  • Import and Export customs clearance support
  • Air Cargo
  • Sea Freight
  • Project / Oversized shipments
  • Oversize shipments
  • Storage/Logistics facilities

We evaluate the available space and capacities in vehicles to minimize unnecessary shipments that are not economically feasible for transporting a product.

Transportation services offered in collaboration with Turkish transport companies we have worked with for many years:

  • Direct road transport
  • Road + Sea Ro-Ro transport

We also offer alternative transportation services to minimize the environmental impact of exhaust gas emissions:

  • Intermodal combined road transport (TRAIN)
  • Intermodal combined road transport (TRAIN) + Sea Ro-Ro (FERRY)

NOTE: All our vehicles are equipped with the latest version of the EURO 6 Emission standard
